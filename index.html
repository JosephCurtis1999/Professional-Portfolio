What is cryptocurrency


CryptoGuide

Cryptocurrency is decentralized digital money designed to be used over the internet. It makes it possible to transfer value online without the requirement for a middleman. Cryptocurrencies are not issued or controlled by any government or central bank.

What is blockchain


CryptoGuide

Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin are powered by a technology called blockchain. Blockchain is a list of transactions that anyone can view and verify. Payments via blockchain can be more secure than standard debit/credit card transactions.

What is Bitcoin


CryptoGuide

Created by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008, Bitcoin is digital money that allows secure peer-to-peer transactions on the internet. Two people, anywhere in the world, can send bitcoin to each other without the involvement of a bank, government or other institution.
